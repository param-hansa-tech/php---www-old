About Param Hansa Philanthropies


Param Hansa Philanthropies (PHP) is Dheeraj and Swapna Pandey's private foundation registered under Section 501(c)(3) of US Internal Revenue Code aiming at improving human life through application of science and technology.


Param Hansa Philanthropies Foundation (PHPF) India is registered as a Section 8 Non-Profit Company and the implementing partner of PHP USA.



Param Hansa derives the name and inspiration from Paramahansa Yogananda. PHP aims to collaborate closely with universities and research organizations in the computing and life sciences sectors. The goal is to contribute to revolutionary ideas that leverage technology to address healthcare challenges and improve society.

PHP collaborates with global institutions, universities and labs to advance ideas that utilize the power of natural sciences for a better future.
