Our Collaborations


At Param Hansa Philanthropies, we are committed to supporting research initiatives with esteemed global institutions, universities, and cutting-edge laboratories to drive progress at the intersection of computing and life sciences.


Our collaboration formats include capital investment, providing venture capital, development capital, private equity and investment funding or venture capital services including providing financing to emerging and start-ups.

By harnessing the transformative potential of technology and natural sciences, we aspire to create a society where advancements in these fields lead to tangible improvements in human health, sustainability, and overall well-being.
