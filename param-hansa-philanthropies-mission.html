Our Mission

At Param Hansa Philanthropies, our mission is to enhance the quality of human life through the power of science, complimented by innovations in technology.


Our vision is to collaborate and support research at the intersection of computing and life sciences besides the ideas that harness the power of natural sciences to create better versions of our society.




At Param Hansa Philanthropies, our focus is on supporting research at the convergence of computing and life sciences. We collaborate with global institutions, universities and labs to advance ideas that utilize the power of natural sciences for a better future.
