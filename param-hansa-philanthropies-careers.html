Shape your future with us!


Through collaboration with global institutions and cutting-edge labs, we offer an environment where your talents can flourish.


Whether seeking full-time roles, aspiring to engage in research, eager for internships, or wanting to volunteer, we provide empowering opportunities to shape the future. Join us to pioneer transformative ideas!

Internships

We're seeking vibrant young minds ready to innovate and make a difference in human lives. Join our PHP team as an intern for a hands-on journey where outstanding performers may transition to full-time roles. Be part of our dual quest to position India as a research leader and solve significant life sciences challenges with revolutionary technology. Your ideas could shape the future - let's create change together!



Full-time Employment

We're seeking graduates with zeal and innovative ideas, having a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Zoology, Botany, Mathematics, Life Sciences, Bioinformatics, or Microbiology. Contribute to PHP's ambitious goals: transforming India into a research hub and tackling vital life sciences challenges through cutting-edge technology. Your role could be pivotal in shaping a new era of scientific inquiry.



Volunteering

Join us as a volunteer and ignite transformation in computational biology research. Plunge into innovation, challenge the conventional, and help advance the frontier of biological discovery with tech's transformative power. Get the opportunity to work along side PHP's core team, and make an impact where pioneering exploration meets passion.


At Param Hansa Philanthropies, our focus is on supporting research at the convergence of computing and life sciences. We collaborate with global institutions, universities and labs to advance ideas that utilize the power of natural sciences for a better future.
