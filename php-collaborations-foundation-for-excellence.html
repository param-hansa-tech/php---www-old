Collaboration with Foundation for Excellence




The Foundation for Excellence and Param Hansa Philanthropies (PHP) partnership (FFE-PHP Scholarship Program) commenced in FY 2022-23.

Under this initiative, PHP has committed to provide scholarships to 32 (16 engineering and 16 medical) meritorious students from challenging financial situations. These students are pursuing 4-year BE/B.Tech (Bachelor in Engineering or Bachelor of Technology) or 5-year MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) program in institutions spread across India.


While the program's focus is providing financial support for qualified students, the assistance extends beyond finances and includes training, mentorship, and other relevant career readiness programs.
